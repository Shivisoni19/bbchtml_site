Energy Ombudsman:

Suppose your complaint remains unresolved after eight weeks or you are dissatisfied with the Business gas prices provided by EDF Energy. In that case, you can escalate the matter to the Energy Ombudsman. This service is impartial and free for energy consumers. They will thoroughly investigate your complaint, and their decision is legally binding.
