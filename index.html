Why compare Energy Prices?

Regularly comparing energy prices goes beyond saving money; it's a gateway to affordability and environmental responsibility. By exploring various energy tariffs, you can uncover cost-effective options, effectively reducing your energy bills. Yet, the true value of this practice extends beyond monetary savings. For those dedicated to minimizing their environmental footprint, opting for a green energy supplier, even with a slightly higher cost, signifies a conscious choice. It's about managing your budget intelligently while actively endorsing renewable energy sources, playing a crucial role in building a cleaner and more sustainable future.

Important info in Energy Comparison

When evaluating various energy plans, there are two key factors to keep in mind. The cost per kWh, which dictates what you pay for each unit of electricity and gas, varies among suppliers, and the standing charge, a daily fee for energy supply, can differ from one provider to another. It's vital to consider both these elements because while a plan may offer a low cost per kWh, it might offset these savings with a higher daily fee, potentially increasing your overall energy expenses. Therefore, a comprehensive assessment of these costs is essential to determine the true value of an energy plan.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I switch my current supplier anytime I want to?

Switching suppliers at any time will charge you with exit fees. However, you can switch without being charged exit fees only when the switching window opens i.e. 49 days before the contract ends.

Is it beneficial to compare and switch business energy?

The main reason for comparing business energy tariffs and switching their respective suppliers is to save some on your increasing energy bills. Here at BBC Utilities, it is free to run an energy comparison in a few minutes.

How does switching energy work today, and is it a long process?

The process is quick and smooth. It's important to understand your current contract terms and any exit fees, as well as to compare the new deal to make sure it's financially beneficial before making the switch. The entire process is relatively short and efficient, allowing you to enjoy better energy rates without much delay. It is a straightforward online process. Once you have signed up with the new supplier, the switch will be completed in a maximum of 3 weeks.

What are smart meters?

Smart meters are electronic devices that measure how much electricity and gas is being consumed by your business via remote connection and send them directly to your supplier.

If I have questions, where can I find answers?

With any queries you may face other than this, you can contact us and we will try to solve every problem you face.

What is a Green Energy Plan?

Some suppliers provide energy that is produced by renewable resources to reduce their carbon footprint, and for a sustainable future.

What information do I need to know to switch Business energy suppliers?

All you need to know to compare and switch your energy supplier is a few details that is, your postcode of your address, your current supplier’s name, your current tariff’s name & end date, and your total consumption.

Will my gas and electricity be cut off if I switch to my current supplier?

No, your energy supply won’t be affected during your switching process. Around 30,000 electricity and 10,000 gas consumers switch every month.
